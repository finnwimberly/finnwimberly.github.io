Projects

  • Cape Cod Ocean Watch
    Led the development of the ccocean dashboard. This project is a collaboration between the Cape Cod Commercial Fishermen's Alliance (CCCFA), Commercial Fisheries Reasearch Fleet (CFRF), NOAA's eMOLT program, and WHOI. The dashboard aggregates oceanographic data from multiple sources spanning the Mid-Atlantic Bight to the Gulf of Maine. We aim to help build knowledge for scientists, make observational data more accessible to a wide range of users, and find connections between changing ocean conditions and fishing success.
